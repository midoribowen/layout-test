Balls

Ball. Did you say ball? Oh, my bad, that was me. Well, anyway, I love balls. I love them in the air and on the ground. But most of all I love them in my mouth.

The sweet, fibrous, flavor of the tennis ball is one of life's grandest joys. I cannot possibly express my gratitude to Penn for making the finest balls the world over. Wilson's balls are also not bad, but they don't bring the specialty of tennis ball-making to the table that Penn does.

Well, that's all I have on balls. For now.

Dead Crab

Today, while at the beach with my human, I had the delight and luck of sampling a dead crab. It was delicious and pungent. Laying in the hot sun, I chomped through the first layer of shell and my teeth squished into the putrid flavor inside. The scent wafted out and tickled my nose hairs.

It was at that moment that my human realized the tasty treat I had unearthed and greedily threw it into the salty water. Undoubtedly, she will return later to feast.
