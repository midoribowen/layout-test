About Me

My name is Musashi. This is my story. I was born on September 27nd, 2010. I lived a hard early life, on the streets of Portland, until two humans imprisoned me amongst 15 other cats. After several incidents of violence against the other captives, I was transferred to a high-security prison, where I was kept in solitary confinement. In MaxOHS there were several escape attempts that ended in me wounding multiple guards and a visit to what they called 'Quarantine'.

After several months of struggle they deemed me dangerous and warned all prospective captors against my violence. Two such humans decided that I would be a good choice for a prisoner and they 'adopted' me.

My new captors operate what I can only guess is a very minimum security prison. I must have gained fame and therefore a cushy imprisonment. My only companion is a turtle, who is kept in a glass cell. She believes me to be an enemy, however sometimes I watch her in hopes that she will tell me more information about our captors.
